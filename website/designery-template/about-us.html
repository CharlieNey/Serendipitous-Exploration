About Us.

Cathy Duan

Hello! My name is Cathy and I’m a senior computer science major from Doylestown, PA. I was interested in this comps project because it’s all about encouraging the liberal arts experience at Carleton, which has helped me discover some of my favorite classes here (from departments like Cinema and Media Studies, American Studies, Sociology, etc.). It was fun to build something that could help the Carleton community and I learned a lot! In my free time, I like to watch movies with friends, go on hikes, and doodle little things on random bits of paper.

Special thanks to our Comps Adviser: Eric Alexander
